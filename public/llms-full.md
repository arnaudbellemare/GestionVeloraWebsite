## Company identity

**Legal name**: Gestion Velora
**Founder**: Arnaud Bellemare
**Founded**: 2025
**Headquarters**: Montréal, QC, Canada (lat 45.5017, lon -73.5673)
**Languages of service**: French (primary), English
**Slogan**: "Votre tranquillité. Notre affaire." / "Your peace of mind. Our business."

Gestion Velora is a property management operator — not a marketplace or directory. The company holds direct management mandates with condo boards (syndicats de copropriété), Airbnb hosts, and residential landlords across Greater Montreal and broader Quebec.

Core operations span: day-to-day administration, tenant and guest relations, maintenance coordination, reserve-fund planning, financial reporting, and regulatory compliance.

Regulatory context: Quebec Civil Code (Book IV – Co-ownership), Act respecting tourist accommodation establishments, TAL (Tribunal administratif du logement), CNESST, Bill 16 / fonds de prévoyance obligations.

## Services

### 1. Condo board management (Syndicat de copropriété)

Full administration of co-ownership associations under Quebec's Civil Code. Covers:
- General assembly coordination and minutes
- Annual budget preparation and fonds de prévoyance (reserve fund) planning per Bill 16
- Contractor oversight and tendering (appels d'offres)
- Owner and board communications
- Regulatory compliance (declarations, insurance, building records)
- Emergency response and incident tracking

**FR**: https://www.gestionvelora.com/services/syndicat-copropriete
**EN**: https://www.gestionvelora.com/en/services/syndicat-copropriete

### 2. Airbnb / short-term rental management

End-to-end operations for Airbnb and short-term rental units in Montreal. Covers:
- Listing creation and optimisation (titles, photos, descriptions)
- Dynamic pricing and calendar management
- Guest communication and review management
- Cleaning and linen coordination
- Permit compliance under Montreal's tourist accommodation rules
- Owner reporting (occupancy, revenue, incidents)

**FR**: https://www.gestionvelora.com/services/airbnb
**EN**: https://www.gestionvelora.com/en/services/airbnb

### 3. Long-term rental management (Gestion locative)

Complete landlord-side management of residential leases. Covers:
- Tenant screening and selection
- Lease preparation (TAL-standard forms)
- Rent collection and arrears follow-up
- Maintenance dispatch and contractor coordination
- Move-in / move-out inspections
- End-of-lease renewals and legal compliance

**FR**: https://www.gestionvelora.com/services/location
**EN**: https://www.gestionvelora.com/en/services/location
