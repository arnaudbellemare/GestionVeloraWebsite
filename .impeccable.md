## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience is mixed: condo boards, property owners/investors, and Airbnb hosts.
They use the site to quickly evaluate if Gestion Velora is trustworthy and capable.
Main jobs to be done are to build trust, explain service scope, and drive qualified calls.

### Brand Personality
Human, serious, and premium.
The interface should feel calm, reliable, and structured, with enough warmth to feel approachable.
Avoid playful or trendy visual noise that weakens credibility.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined tech-minimal with subtle luxury cues.
Use clear hierarchy, strong contrast, and restrained accents.
Avoid generic SaaS patterns, decorative clutter, and overly playful components.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with credibility: clarity and trust cues first, decoration second.
2. Keep interactions quiet and confident: smooth, minimal motion with fast feedback.
3. Use visual restraint: fewer but stronger design moves with consistent spacing and type rhythm.
4. Maintain business tone with human warmth in copy and interaction details.
5. Every section should help conversion: proof, clarity, and a clear next step.
