# Service Summary - Long-Term Rental Management

## FR
Ce service couvre la gestion locative longue duree: commercialisation, selection des locataires, baux, suivi des loyers, maintenance, relation locataire et gestion des situations sensibles.

Objectif principal: stabiliser l'occupation, proteger la tresorerie et maintenir la valeur des actifs.

## EN
This service covers long-term rental management: marketing, tenant screening, lease lifecycle support, rent follow-up, maintenance, tenant relations, and sensitive-case handling.

Primary objective: stabilize occupancy, protect cash flow, and maintain long-term asset value.
