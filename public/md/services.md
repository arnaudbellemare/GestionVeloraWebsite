## 2. Airbnb / Short-Term Rental Management / Gestion Airbnb

**FR:** Gestion integrale des locations courtes durees a Montreal. Annonces, tarification, operations, conformite reglementaire et communication avec les voyageurs.

**EN:** End-to-end Airbnb and short-term rental management in Montreal. Listings, dynamic pricing, operations, regulatory compliance, and guest communication.

### What is included:
- Listing creation and optimization on Airbnb and other platforms
- Dynamic pricing based on seasonality and local events
- Check-in / check-out coordination (autonomous or assisted)
- Professional cleaning with quality checklists between stays
- Maintenance and 24/7 emergency management
- CITQ certificate and municipal permit compliance
- Guest communication and review management
- Revenue and performance reporting

### Airbnb regulations in Montreal (2025-2026):
- A CITQ certificate is mandatory and must appear on all listings
- Primary residences: permit required, authorized June 10 to September 10
- Non-primary residences: year-round operation in authorized zones with an occupancy certificate
- Municipal rules and provincial requirements change regularly; ongoing compliance monitoring is essential

### Ideal for: Owners of one or more units who want to maximize short-term rental income without managing day-to-day operations.
