Methodology

Gestion Velora uses a repeatable operating framework for property management mandates in Montreal.

1) Baseline assessment

We evaluate occupancy, maintenance backlog, response times, recurring vendor costs, and compliance checkpoints relevant to each asset type.

2) KPI dashboard

  • Occupancy rate and vacancy duration
  • Response time and work-order closure time
  • Preventive versus emergency maintenance ratio
  • Budget variance and owner cash-flow visibility

3) Compliance workflow

We map legal and administrative obligations to checklists and calendar controls. For short-term rentals, this includes permit and registry requirements. For condos, this includes reserve-fund and governance obligations.

4) Iteration cycle

We run monthly operational reviews and recommend adjustments based on measured outcomes. The objective is stable occupancy, fewer avoidable emergencies, and better long-term planning.
