Editorial Policy

This page describes how Gestion Velora writes and updates educational content about property management in Montreal and Quebec.

Scope

Content covers condo board management, long-term rental operations, short-term rental compliance, maintenance, and owner reporting. Content is informational and does not replace legal or tax advice.

Source standards

  • Prefer primary sources from Quebec and Montreal public institutions.
  • Use source links close to factual claims whenever possible.
  • Mark publication and update dates for major pages and articles.

Review and updates

Articles are reviewed when regulations or operating conditions change. We update pages with material changes and maintain clear links to current source pages.

Corrections

If you identify an error, contact info@gestionvelora.com. We review reports and correct verified inaccuracies.
